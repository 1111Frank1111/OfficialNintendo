Their headquarters are located: Kyoto, Kyoto Prefecture, Japan
Nintendo of America (NOA), is the american division of Nintendo. It is located in Redmond, Washington (next to Seattle). First location was in New York City, and still have offices located at 445 Park Avenue. Nintendo is also located in other countries like Canada and Germany.

Nintendo was invented in 23 September 1889 (Date founded).
The console was initially released in Japan as the Family Computer (Famicom) in 1983.
The original Nintendo come out was called the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) is an 8-bit video game console which was released in Europe throughout 1986 and 1987 and in North America in 1985.
Nintendo’s products like the Wii and the 3DS are assembled at Foxconn plants. The Wii U parts manufactured in Japan, but the final assembly is done in China. Nintendo hasn't manufactured hardware in Japan in many years.
Nintendo was founded by Fusajiro Yamauchi on September 23, 1889, as Nintendo Koppai. Based in Kyoto Japan as a card business.

Also a well known company for their handheld consoles like their Game & Watch series, Game Boy’s, DS’ s and 3DS’s. And also their home consoles like their NES, SNES, N64, GCN, Wii, Wii U and Switch.
